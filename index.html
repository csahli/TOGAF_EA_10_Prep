About this tool

A self-study practice environment modeled on the format of the TOGAF® EA Practitioner certification (TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition).

  • Level 1 (Foundation): single-best-answer recall questions.
  • Level 2 (Practitioner): scenario questions with gradient scoring (best = 5, second-best = 3, others = 0).
  • Blended: mixes both levels in one session.

⚠️ All questions are original study material. They are not real exam questions and reproduce no proprietary Open Group content. For authentic practice exams use The Open Group or accredited training providers. TOGAF® is a registered trademark of The Open Group.
