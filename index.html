About this tool

A self-study practice environment modeled on the format of the TOGAF® EA Practitioner certification (TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition).

  • Level 1 (Foundation): single-best-answer recall questions, every item referenced back to the C220 evaluation bundle.
  • Level 2 (Practitioner): scenario questions with the official OGEA-102 gradient: best = 5, second-best = 3, third-best = 1, distractor = 0.
  • Blended: mixes both levels in one practice session.
  • Official exam simulation: enforces the published OGEA-101 (40 q / 60 min / closed book), OGEA-102 (8 q / 90 min / open book) or OGEA-103 combined (48 q / 150 min) structure with a visible timer. Pass mark is 60% in each part.

⚠️ All questions are original study material. They are not real exam questions and reproduce no proprietary Open Group content. For authentic practice exams use The Open Group or accredited training providers. TOGAF® is a registered trademark of The Open Group.
